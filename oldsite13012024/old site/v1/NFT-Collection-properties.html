NFT Collections

All NFTs will be tradable on any upcoming NFT Marketplace on Andromeda mainnet and on the BTE platform itself.

BTE Primary Heroes

Total supply: 100
ERC-721 standard Dynamic NFT Collection.
NFT Attributes:
   - Attack
   - Defense
   - Knowledge
   - Spell Power
   - HP

The metadata stored on-chain which allows the game to update the look and attributes of the NFT once a Hero level up.
Anyone who hold NFTs from this collection can mint BTE Secondary Heroes using BTE Gold Token.

BTE Primary Creatures

Total supply: 100
ERC-721 standard Dynamic NFT Collection.
NFT Attributes:
   - Attack
   - Defense
   - HP

Anyone who hold NFTs from this collection can mint BTE Secondary Creatures using BTE Gold Token.

BTE Artifacts

Total supply: 150
ERC-1155 standard NFT Collection.
NFT Attributes:
   - Attack
   - Defense
   - Knowledge
   - Spell Power
   - HP


BTE Artifacts are increasing the attributes of the Hero who holds it (during the battles)
Initially BTE Artifacts can be acquired only using BTE Marketplace and BTE Gold Token. Once a user purchased the NFT it can be sold on any NFT Marketplace on Andromeda mainnet for their supported tokens.

BTE Secondary Creatures

Secondary Creatures have the same attributes as Primary ones with the exception that they cannot mint new Creatures.

BTE Regions

Total supply: 50

BTE Regions are land pieces of Erathia Continent. Players can purchase a land and earn BTE Gold Tokens for every other player who wonders on the land.
