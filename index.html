Back to Erathia

web3 - NFT - Game

Backtoerathia offers a unique passive gaming experience for all web3 fans who enjoy the warmth of nostalgy. It is inspired by the greatest game in the history of mankind: Heroes of Might and Magic III.

Dragons NFT (2000)

Each NFT is a unique dragon which belongs to one of 8 teams (Red, blue, green, tan, orange, pink, teal and purple) and numerous unique attributes  

Heroes NFT (500)

BTE Heroes are the first dynamic NFT collection on Metis Andromeda. Heroes can gain experience every time a dragon in their team takes part in a fight. By gaining experience Heroes can emerge to new levels increasing the default attributes.
Each BTE Dragon inherits part of the Heroes attributes during the fights, thus a mutual benefit and bond between the two collection.

DragonPit

Dragon Pit is an NFT based passive game built on Metis.
By owning BTE Dragons holders are automatically enrolling their majestic creatures to fight other dragons.

 Each week a war takes place where teams fight in daily battles in random order. The first four days are the qualifier rounds, the fifth and sixth are semi finals and the seventh day is the final battle.

 When the tournament starts two teams are matched up and dragons from each team are randomly paired up in a one on one fight using attack, defense, HP and luck attributes. One dragon wins and one loses. The team with more collective wins is declared the winner and moves on to the next battle.(See example on the above).
